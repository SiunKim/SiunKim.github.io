Projects 15 projects · 2018–2025

  • Global Data Convergence Leader Training Program — practical management · Seoul National University Hospital (2024.8 – 2025.12). General manager overseeing research committee activities, student research supervision, and development of clinical text generation and medical language models.
  • Quantitative evaluation of medical QA benchmarks for clinical LLM capabilities · SNUH (2025.2 – 2025.8). Investigating the limitations of medical licensing exams as evaluation metrics for clinical LLMs.
  • Zero-shot NER via disagreement signals across multiple LLMs (2023.3 – 2025.10). Model-iteration configuration selection outperforming instruction fine-tuned baselines.
  • LLM-based automation of systematic literature reviews, validated on Cochrane reviews (2023.3 – 2025.10). Pipeline achieving ~90% recall with improved reproducibility.
  • Key patient-information extraction from Hematology-Oncology clinical notes (2023.3 – 2025.10). Extracting diagnoses, symptoms, and prescriptions using few-shot retrieval and LLM in-context learning.
  • Emergency-department case retrieval system based on clinical guidelines · SNUH (2024.3 – 2025.12).
  • Eating alone as a mediator for depression in one-person households (2025.1 – 2025.12). Epidemiological analysis using KNHANES data.
  • Intra-dialysis hypotension (IDH) prediction from TDMS time-series vital-sign and dialyzer data · SNUH (2023.3 – 2025.8). Lead researcher.
  • Revisiting ML-based drug repositioning with clinical-trial eligibility criteria · Microsoft Research Asia (2022.5 – 2023.4). Reformulation of ML-based drug repositioning with eligibility-criteria recommendation.
  • Prospective real-world registry for pediatric ALL from multi-institutional EHRs · Korea Health Industry Development Institute (2022.3 – 2022.12). Report-form design and extraction-algorithm development.
  • NLP algorithms for extracting key clinical information from drug adverse-event reports · Ministry of Food and Drug Safety (2021.2 – 2022.2). Project lead.
  • Target Product Profile (TPP) of REX101, a bacterial-exosome anticancer therapy · Rosetta Exosome (2021.3 – 2021.12). Lead researcher.
  • IDX-1197 development strategy consultation · Idience Co., Ltd (2020.10 – 2021.2). Pre-clinical and clinical database construction for PARP inhibitors.
  • Drug-interaction evaluation using big data and machine learning · Ministry of Food and Drug Safety (2018.2 – 2022.2). NLP for drug-food interaction extraction and drug-drug interaction prediction.
  • PK/PD data analysis for Phase 1 trials of YH12852 and CWP232291 · Seoul National University (2018.3 – 2022.12). Analysis resulting in a population PK/PD model publication.
